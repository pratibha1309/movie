Season 1

"Peaky Blinders" is a British period crime drama series set in the aftermath of World War I, following the exploits of the Peaky Blinders crime gang in Birmingham, England. Led by the fierce and enigmatic Tommy Shelby, the gang is involved in various criminal activities, including theft, extortion, and illegal gambling. The series unfolds as the Peaky Blinders navigate the treacherous criminal underworld and encounter formidable adversaries, including rival gangs and law enforcement. The show's unique elements, such as its captivating sets and the distinctive fashion of the 1920s and 1930s, have contributed to its status as a cultural phenomenon. "Peaky Blinders" first aired in 2013 and has garnered a dedicated global following, with its compelling storytelling and memorable characters leaving a lasting impact on audiences
