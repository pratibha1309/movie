More About Suits

Season 1

Season 1 of "Suits" introduces viewers to Mike Ross, a brilliant college dropout with a photographic memory, who finds himself in a job interview with one of New York City's best legal closers, Harvey Specter. Despite his lack of a law degree, Mike impresses Harvey with his wit and knowledge, leading to an unconventional hiring. The season follows Mike as he navigates the high-stakes legal world while keeping his secret, and Harvey as he takes on cases and deals with personal and professional challenges. The dynamic between the two central characters, along with the engaging legal cases and the ensemble cast, sets the stage for the show's successful run. "Suits" Season 1 originally aired on USA Network from June 23 to September 8, 2011, and received positive reviews for its sharp writing, engaging performances, and compelling storytelling
